ECAM Solar Racing Team

About us

We are a team of passionate engineering students preparing to participate to the IESC which is an electrical car race where students build their car so that it can race for 24 hour. We aim to take part in the 2028 edition as the first french speaking engineering school in Belgium setting standard in terms of sustainable car race.
