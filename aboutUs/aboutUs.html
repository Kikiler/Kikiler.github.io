About us

We are a team of passionate engineering students preparing to participate to the IESC which is an electrical car race where students build their car so that it can race for 24 hour. We aim to take part in the 2028 edition as the first french speaking engineering school in Belgium setting standard in terms of sustainable car race.

Our school has a long story of participating to mechanical car races such as ECO Shell as well as being renowned for producing hands-on result-oriented electromechanical engineers. As the the awareness about our ecological situation grew, our school slowly decided to cut bands with projects that didn't align with the desire of our school to produce not only result-oriented and qualified engineers but also those who will be able to tackle the challenges of tomorrow regarding sustainable development.

This project perfectly combines technical challenges in various domains allowing student from different majors to participate.
