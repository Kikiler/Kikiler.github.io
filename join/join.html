Recruiting — Season 01

Show up with curiosity. Leave knowing how to build an electrical race car.

No experience needed. No GPA cutoff. If you're willing to get your hands dirty, we'll learn together from torque specs to building solar panels. Here's what you get when you join the crew.

🔥

Welding & fabrication

MIG, TIG, plasma cutting and sheet-metal work. By mid 2027 you'll be making parts that bolt onto a 50 km/h car.
