Recruiting — Season 01

Show up with curiosity. Leave knowing how to build a race car.

No experience needed. No GPA cutoff. If you're willing to get your hands dirty, we'll teach you everything from torque specs to apex theory. Here's what you get when you join the crew.

🔧

Build a real race car

Learn engine swaps, suspension geometry, brake systems and roll cage fab — on an actual logbooked car, not a textbook.

🔥

Welding & fabrication

MIG, TIG, plasma cutting and sheet-metal work. By spring you'll be making parts that bolt onto a 90mph car.

⚡

Wiring & electronics

Harness building, ECU tuning, data logging and telemetry. Walk away with skills that pay $40+/hr in any shop.

🏁

Get track time

Every member gets seat time in the sim rig and a passenger lap on track day. Drivers earn their seat through the program.

🎓

College & scholarships

We help you turn the build into a portfolio for engineering programs. Past members got into Purdue, Clemson and Michigan.

🚐

Travel with the crew

Road trips to regional rounds, paddock passes, and pit lane access. Food and fuel covered by the team budget.

👕

Free team kit

Crew shirt, fire-retardant gloves, and your name stitched on the door once you've earned your first 50 shop hours.

🤝

A crew that has your back

Tutoring, mentorship from alumni working at OEMs, and a group chat that never sleeps. You're not alone in this.

Ready to wrench?

Drop by the metal shop after 6th period any Tuesday or Thursday. No appointment needed — just bring closed-toe shoes.
